Shipping and Delivery

Your guide to our shipping policies and delivery timelines

Shipping Policy

We are committed to ensuring that your order reaches you safely and promptly. Our shipping policy provides details about the process, timelines, and charges.

Shipping Charges

  • Standard Shipping: Free on orders above ₹4,000. A flat rate of ₹300 applies to orders below this amount.
  • Express Shipping: ₹1,000 for expedited delivery within 1-2 business days.

Delivery Timelines

Delivery times vary based on the destination and shipping method chosen at checkout.

  • Domestic: Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, while express options take 1-2 business days.
  • International: Depending on the destination, delivery may take 7-14 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, a tracking number will be shared via email. You can use this to monitor your delivery status.
